Parallel bidirectional tree expansion guided by Bayesian Optimization over sliding windows.

Abstract

We present BOWConnect, a parallel bidirectional kinodynamic motion planner that integrates Bayesian Optimization over sliding windows within a tree-based exploration framework. By learning local Gaussian Process cost maps over control inputs, BOWConnect efficiently guides sampling toward dynamically feasible, collision-free trajectories.

Extensive experiments across UGV and UAV platforms demonstrate 100% success in challenging environments while achieving significantly lower computation time compared to state-of-the-art kinodynamic planners.

Method Overview

BOWConnect spawns multiple forward and backward workers that grow trees in parallel. Each worker performs constrained Bayesian Optimization over control windows, learning local cost maps that accelerate kinodynamic expansion. Spatial hashing enables efficient connection detection, and a boundary value solver generates dynamically feasible bridges.
